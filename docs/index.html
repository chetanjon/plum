First open

Homebrew works too: brew install --cask chetanjon/chalant/chalant. macOS will ask once: System Settings, Privacy and Security, Open Anyway. That is the only ceremony there will ever be: from then on the island mentions a new version once and one click installs it in place. Chalant is unsigned because it is free and independent. The source is public, speech recognition uses Apple standard dictation, and Chalant itself only asks the internet whether a newer version exists, and for the update when you say yes. The optional Chat tab is a small built-in browser where you sign in to Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini with your own account; Chalant is not affiliated with Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google. Everything can be switched off.
